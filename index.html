Customer Segmentation

Software to recognize customer segmentation

In this application, I just categorize 3 type of customer (Regular, Potential and VIP) but I believe it can be developed into more than 3 types.Customers who have the highest total purchases will be grouped into VIPs. Customers who have the lowest total purchases will be grouped into Regulars. And the ones who have the total purchases between highest and lowest will be Potential.Total purchases of customers will be the parameter to decided the segmentation.
